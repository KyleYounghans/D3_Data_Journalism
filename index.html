Age (Median)

  • Since healthcare is generally tied to a job, age may impact having healthcare, however it doesn't appear to be strongly correlated.
  • Age doesn't appear to have a correlation to obesity percentage.
  • Age doesn't appear to have a correlation to smoking percentage.

Household Income (Median)

  • Since healthcare is generally tied to a job, income may impact having healthcare, however it doesn't appear to be strongly correlated.
  • Income appears to be correlated to obesity. Higher income looks to correlate with lower obesity percentage.
  • Income appears to be correlated to smoking. Higher income looks to correlate with lower smoking percentage.

In Poverty (%)

  • Since healthcare is tied to a job, poverty percentage appears to be strongly correlated.
  • As with income, poverty percentage appears to be correlated to obesity. Higher poverty percentage shows higher obesity percentage.
  • As with income, poverty percentage appears to be correlated to smoking. Higher poverty percentage shows higher smoking percentage.
